About

Making the world’s information more accessible.

Visual Capitalist uses powerful visual content to help investors and business professionals understand the world. Focusing on topics such as markets, technology, energy, and the global economy, Visual Capitalist is currently one of the fastest growing online publishers in North America.
The company’s approach of fusing data, art, and storytelling has helped build an affluent millennial audience (>50%) with a reach that is expanding rapidly. Visual Capitalist’s work can also often be found in Business Insider, Marketwatch, Zero Hedge, and other business-oriented publications

Leadership

Jeff Desjardins, Founder and Editor-in-Chief

Jeff believes the fusion of information and design can deliver new insights to investors on markets and the economy. Jeff holds a BCom degree from the Sauder School of Business at the University of British Columbia.
He is a regular contributor to the World Economic Forum and Business Insider and his work has also been featured on Forbes, CNBC, MarketWatch, Huffington Post, Fast Company, BNN and TechCrunch.
